About

ICAN is a model, a framework to raise our standard.
It involves the principle of Kaizen and allows us to build our potential in doing any kind of activity that can be measured using count or time constraint.



  • REFERENCE POINT

    In ancient times, American companies were rebounding, they realized Japanese companies were successful. Japanese used to live by a philosophy of ‘kaizen’, that means never ending improvement. They used to keep on asking kaizen in business, kaizen in customers, kaizen in products. If we generalize it is only one question, ‘How can we improve?’

  • The BASIC IDEA

    Every great accomplishment starts with taking the first step and gradually adding increments that collectively produce the result. Ican Model will help you accomplish this result with the right principles and insight !

  • SIX HUMAN NEEDS

    ICAN model can be explained by the six emotional human needs given by TONY ROBBINS . We don’t want our goals and desires, we only want our needs to be met, and we see our goals as a way to fulfill our needs. We can even drop our goals for our needs to be met.

  • SIX HUMAN NEEDS FOR ICAN

    ICAN model fulfills the six human needs in a powerful manner which explains why this model will work in favour for us !

  • WHAT MAKES ICAN A SUCCESS ?

    It talks about the eight principles and helps us to take control over them and master them with time !
